Built for what comes after the AI hype

Most AI deployments today are point solutions — disconnected tools that answer questions but don't coordinate action. Critical Insight Inc. exists to solve the harder problem: how do you build AI systems that operate with the structure, memory, and governance that enterprise work actually demands?

Our answer is CognOS — a cognitive operating system that gives organizations the runtime infrastructure to deploy intelligent, adaptive, auditable workloads across every domain in their business.

Platform Architecture

Three pillars. One cognitive system.

CognOS is built around three independent modules that unify into a single, governed intelligence runtime.

Pillar 01

Orchestration

Coordinates long-running, stateful work across autonomous agents, workflows, and processes — with cognitive memory systems that give agents context, episodic recall, and procedural knowledge.

Pillar 02

Knowledge Mesh

A live, queryable knowledge graph that stores entities, claims, and embeddings with full provenance tracking. Exposes graph, search, and analytics query surfaces to any agent or workflow.

Pillar 03

Event Fabric

Manages real-time data movement through typed streams, composable pipelines, and reactive triggers — bridging internal agent coordination with external system integration.
